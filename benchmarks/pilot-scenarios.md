# Pilot Scenario Ideas

## 1. Startup Operating Review

**Premise:** A seed-stage startup needs a monthly operating review for its board.

**Sources:** investor updates, Stripe export, support tickets, hiring plan, CRM CSV, founder Slack snippets, customer interview notes.

**Deliverables:** KPI spreadsheet, board memo, risk register.

**Why useful:** common, accessible, realistic; can be synthetic with public SaaS metrics patterns.

## 2. Nonprofit Grant Strategy

**Premise:** A nonprofit wants to decide which grants to pursue.

**Sources:** public grant PDFs, program descriptions, budget spreadsheet, prior annual report, staff notes.

**Deliverables:** grant-fit matrix, application outline, executive memo.

**Why useful:** sponsor-friendly, public-good angle, lower IP risk.

## 3. Product Launch Postmortem

**Premise:** A product launch underperformed and leadership wants root cause and next actions.

**Sources:** analytics CSV, support tickets, mock Slack threads, product requirements doc, release notes, survey responses.

**Deliverables:** postmortem memo, prioritized roadmap, deck.

**Why useful:** exposes causal reasoning, contradictory signals, stakeholder tradeoffs.

## 4. Local Business Diligence

**Premise:** A small PE/search-fund buyer evaluates a local services business.

**Sources:** public market data, synthetic P&L, customer reviews, diligence call transcript, employee roster.

**Deliverables:** investment memo, market model, questions list.

**Why useful:** close to AA-Briefcase-Lite but easier to construct.
