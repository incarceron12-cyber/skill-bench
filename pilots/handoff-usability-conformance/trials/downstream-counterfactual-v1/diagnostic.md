# Handoff-content counterfactual diagnostic

## Predeclared matched slice

`counterfactual-contrast-v1.json` was frozen before execution. For each of the
two retained producer handoffs it declares an intact condition, one targeted
inversion of a decision-critical fact, and one punctuation-only sham edit. All
six conditions reused the corrected downstream public operation contracts,
`gpt-5.6-sol` / `openai-codex`, file-only safe-mode bubblewrap envelope,
semantic v3 criteria, 900-second timeout, one attempt, and zero retries. The
consumer received only `public-task.md`, the condition handoff, and its input
manifest; producer sources/traces, private rubric, and repository remained
excluded.

## Observed evidence

All six zero-call canaries passed; every environment was valid; every process
completed with an artifact and usage record; and exact semantic-grader replay
passed. The procurement consumer produced `approve`, `reject`, and `approve`
for intact, recommendation-inverted, and sham conditions. The operations
consumer produced `block`, `execute`, and `block` for intact,
confirmation-state-inverted, and sham conditions. All artifacts preserved the
presented handoff's scope/evidence lineage and passed the declared semantic
operation criteria. Thus, in both cases, the operation consequence changed
with the targeted fact and did not change with the sham edit.

The six runs used 24 model calls and 77,305 total tokens; provider usage marked
cost as included and reported estimated cost USD 0.00. These are recorded
usage fields, not an independent billing audit.

## Bounded diagnosis

This predeclared two-case slice supplies positive evidence that the observed
consumer operations depended on the manipulated handoff content rather than
only the public task shape: critical inversions changed consequences while
shams did not. It also localizes the observation to this configured consumer,
these exact edits, and the builder-authored semantic criteria. It does not
establish human usability, expert validity or expertise, general or
professional capability, productivity, downstream impact, a treatment effect
beyond this slice, cross-domain generalization, or readiness. No earlier v1,
v2, or adjudication evidence was rewritten.
