## Why this is distinct

The reusable validity chain is:

`source/participant authority → captured proposition and evidence locator → graph relation/version → routing rule and opportunity → recipient access and interpretation → semantic adoption → changed analysis or decision → experimental action/result → burden, privacy, and scientific consequence`.

A narrated sequence in one campaign may support workflow feasibility while leaving routing causality, tacit-knowledge transfer, team irreducibility, scientific validity, counterfactual utility, and transport unestablished. The review should test whether event-level provenance, alternative explanations, non-routed/monolithic comparators, participant roles and consent, graph/routing configuration, traces, downstream experiment records, and complete denominators are inspectable.
