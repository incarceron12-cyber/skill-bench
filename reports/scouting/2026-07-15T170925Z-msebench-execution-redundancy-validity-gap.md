## Why this is distinct

The reusable chain is `public task and admissible state → initial scope estimate → evidence/file/tool acquisition → minimum viable action → verification evidence → justified expansion or stop → artifact/state outcome → complete resource ledger`. Endpoint success alone cannot distinguish calibrated economy from lucky under-inspection, while extensive inspection can be prudent when hidden dependencies or severe consequences exist. A minimum-cost oracle can also encode one author's preferred path and punish legitimate alternatives.

A deep review should therefore separate task success, scope-estimation quality, acquired evidence, verification coverage, expansion triggers, alternate valid paths, environment/provider invalidity, and cost. It should test whether `C_min` is independently warranted, whether the denominator makes ACRR unstable on tiny tasks, whether the deterministic simulator removes capability by authoring the answer into the environment, and whether the live-model case supplies enough independent variation to support transport beyond the controlled probe.
