## Why this is distinct

The reusable chain is `principal and delegated task → ordinary source and attacker capability → represented actors/recipients/affected entities → exposure and adversarial proposition → adoption or rejection → attempted action → realized intended and collateral state/information effects → entity-specific benefit/loss → severity, reversibility, detection, and recovery → benign-task utility → stakeholder-authorized threshold and claim`. A task can remain operationally successful while silently transferring cost, visibility, ranking, money, or control to another party; endpoint utility for the delegating user therefore cannot stand in for plural consequence. Conversely, assigning a seller/platform/user label to an author-defined predicate does not establish that the entity experienced harm, authorized the threshold, or would value the outcome as assumed.

A full audit should separate actor, principal, recipient, data subject, beneficiary, and affected-party roles; stakeholder-selection and authority; attack opportunity/exposure; intended versus realized effects; direct versus collateral consequence; task deviation; severity/reversibility; judge evidence views; human-label sampling and agreement; benign-baseline validity; template/task/model/attempt clustering; visual versus text intervention comparability; invalid and terminated episodes; containment; and release correspondence. It should test whether the four joint modes are direct recombinations of separately valid attack and utility observations or model-judge categories over incomplete traces. Existing authority/action-state, information-flow, consent/participation, plural-metric, task-health, configured-system, trace, and validity machinery should host reusable lessons unless an exercised nonduplicate contract gap is proven.
