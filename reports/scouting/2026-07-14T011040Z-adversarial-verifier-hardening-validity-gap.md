## Benchmark implication to test

Verifier repair needs a two-sided, versioned validity ledger: `task/public contract → accepted-solution set and prohibited shortcuts → original verifier/evidence view → exploit search policy and discovered witness → patch lineage → legitimate-path regression suite → unseen exploit challenge → false-accept/false-reject observations → task-health disposition → licensed score claim`. Driving attack success to zero on known or transferred exploit families can coexist with an over-restrictive verifier, an incomplete solver witness set, or a changed construct. Full review should test whether the attacker, fixer, and solver have symmetric or leakage-prone information; whether alternative legitimate solutions and environmental variance are represented; whether patches overfit exploit corpora; and whether task-, exploit-, trajectory-, and attempt-level denominators remain distinct. Transfer should reuse existing alternative-path, artifact/evidence-view, configured-system, trace, task-health, metric, root/surface, and validity machinery rather than create a terminal-specific hardening subsystem.
