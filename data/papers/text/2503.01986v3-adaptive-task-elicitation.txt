Require: Target model M, seed dataset Q with answers A, profiler_model, verifier_model, diver-
  sity_check.
Ensure: A set of new questions Qnew and a model_profile for M’s.
  1) Seed Evaluation
  Evaluate M on Q to get chain-of-thought traces Rseed and answers Âseed .
  Store (q, A, Âseed , Rseed ) for each q ∈ Q.
  2) Iterative Adaptive Generation
  for each iteration i ∈ {1, . . . , N } do
     Select a subset of in-context examples from Q, including both correctly and incorrectly answered
     questions.
     Use profiler_model to generate a new question qnew based on the selected context.
     Assess qnew for correctness using verifier_model and ensure sufficient novelty using diversity_check.

    if qnew satisfies correctness and diversity constraints then
       Append qnew to Qnew .
       Evaluate M on qnew to obtain Rnew and predicted answer Ânew .
       Update model_profile to reflect newly identified reasoning patterns and weaknesses.
    end if
  end for
  Output: Qnew , Rnew , and updated model_profile.
